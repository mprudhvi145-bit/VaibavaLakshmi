## 2. Design Language & Attributes

### Fabrics
- **Premium:** Raw Silk, Velvet, Banarasi Brocade.
- **Comfort:** Art Silk, Cotton-Silk Blends, Linen.
- **Texture:** Jacquard (Self-design) is preferred over heavy print for a premium look.

### Color Palette
- **Weddings:** Cream, Ivory, Beige, Gold (The classic groom).
- **Reception:** Midnight Blue, Charcoal Black, Deep Wine (Evening wear).
- **Day Functions:** Pista Green, Peach, Dusty Pink, Mustard (Mehendi/Haldi).

## 3. Visual Direction (AI Prompts)
To replace real photography, we use prompts that emphasize:
- **Lighting:** "Cinematic", "Studio Lighting", "Golden Hour".
- **Pose:** "Confident", "Regal", "Adjusting cuff".
- **Background:** "Palace Archway", "Luxury Hotel Lobby", "Minimalist Beige Studio".

## 4. SEO Strategy
- **Titles:** `{Color} {Fabric} {Style} for {Occasion}`
  - *Ex:* "Ivory Raw Silk Embroidered Sherwani for Groom"
- **Keywords:** *Wedding Sherwani, Indo-Western for Men, Kurta Pajama Set, Bandhgala Suit, Festive Wear.*
