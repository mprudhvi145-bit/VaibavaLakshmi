# Product Intelligence Report

**Source Analysis:** Vaibava Lakshmi (Instagram) & Manyavar (Public Assortment)
**Date:** October 2023

## 1. Visual Aesthetics & Design Language

### Women (Vaibava Lakshmi Influence)
- **Core Aesthetic:** Unapologetically traditional South Indian grandeur.
- **Key Colors:** Deep Crimson, Mustard Yellow, Bottle Green, Royal Blue, Rani Pink.
- **Motifs:** Temple borders (Korvai), Peacocks (Mayil), Chakrams, heavy gold Zari.
- **Fabric Focus:** Pure Mulberry Silk (Kanchipuram), Soft Silk (for younger demographic), Banarasi Georgette (for receptions).
- **Vibe:** "The Golden Bride" – heavy emphasis on gold jewelry pairing.

### Men (Manyavar Influence)
- **Core Aesthetic:** Regal, polished, contemporary ethnic.
- **Key Colors:** Cream/Ivory (Weddings), Pastel Green/Peach (Day functions), Navy/Black (Reception/Sangeet).
- **Silhouettes:** Asymmetrical hems on Kurtas, structured Bandhgalas, layered Sherwanis with stoles.
- **Vibe:** "Modern Maharaj" – sophisticated, not overly loud, focus on fabric texture over print.

## 3. Buying Patterns & Keywords
- **Wedding Season:** High demand for "Muhurtham Silk", "Couple Sets", "Haldi Yellow".
- **Keywords:** *Korvai, Zari, Tissue, Butta, Brocade, Pastel, Indo-western, Pathani.*

## 4. Image Direction for AI Generation
To replicate this look without actual photography:
1.  **Lighting:** Warm, golden hour for outdoors; High-contrast "Rembrandt" lighting for studio.
2.  **Backgrounds:** Ancient stone pillars (Temple look), Marigold decor (Festive), textured beige walls (Studio).
3.  **Models:** South Indian features for sarees; Pan-Indian sharp features for men's ethnic.
